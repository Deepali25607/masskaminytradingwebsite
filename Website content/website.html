About Maa Kaminy Trading

Maa Kaminy Trading is a pioneering B2B platform revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain. We connect farmers directly with retailers, restaurants, and institutions, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fresh produce reaches consumers faster and more efficiently.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with better market access and provide businesses with consistent access to quality, fresh produce.

Our Vision

To build the most efficient and transparent agri-supply chain ecosystem globally, fostering sustainable growth for all stakeholders.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Sustainability, Customer Focus, and Community Empowerment.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to optimize every step of the agri-supply chain.

Direct Sourcing

Connecting businesses directly with a vast network of farmers, ensuring fresh, high-quality produce.

Efficient Logistics

Optimized cold chain and transportation to minimize waste and ensure timely delivery.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality checks at every stage to guarantee the freshest and safest produce.

Technology Platform

An intuitive platform for seamless ordering, tracking, and supply chain management.

Market Insights

Data-driven insights to help farmers and businesses make informed decisions.

Farmer Support

Dedicated support and resources for farmers to improve yield and profitability.
